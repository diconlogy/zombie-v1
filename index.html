Frequently Asked Questions

Are their secondary sale royalties?

Yes, royalties are set at 5%. 25% of those royalties go to the Treasury and will be re-invested to sweep the floor as well as funding community decisions of the DAO.

Do I own the Zombie Ape after purchasing?

Yes, of course! Full rights are given to the owner of the wallet address that minted the Zombie Ape.

Is there a limit to how many I can mint?

No.

How do I know this is legit?
